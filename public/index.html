Chapter Four

Where Harvest Road Sits

The home decor influencer space is massive — but there's a clear gap that Harvest Road fills perfectly.

Harvest Road Homes — The Gap

Vintage + New, styled by a real person in a real home. Not a million-dollar renovation. Not a retail store. Not staged perfection. The warm middle ground where French farmhouse meets English cottage, where a $8 thrift find sits next to a Target lamp and it all makes sense.

This space is shockingly underserved at the micro-influencer level.

Big Renovation Accounts

@joannagaines, @studiomcgee

  • Massive budgets, full house renos
  • Aspirational but not replicable
  • Brand deals, product lines

Retail/Product Accounts

@antiquefarmhouse, @potterybarn

  • Selling products, not lifestyle
  • No personal story or face
  • Transactional relationship

Pure Vintage/Thrift

@thriftedabode, @goodwillfinds

  • Focused on the hunt, not the styling
  • Less refined aesthetic
  • Missing the "mix old + new" angle

Modern Farmhouse

@lizmariegalvan, @ourfauxfarmhouse

  • More "modern" than vintage
  • Less French/English cottage influence
  • Different aesthetic lane entirely

"Harvest Road is the warm, collected, story-driven voice in a space dominated by either massive budgets or pure thrift. That's the gap — and it's wide open."

Chapter Eight

Platform Strategy

Instagram builds the community. TikTok brings the strangers.

Instagram

Your curated gallery & loyal community

2026 Algorithm Signals

Watch Time

How long people watch, including replays. The #1 ranking signal.

Sends

DM shares tell Instagram your content is worth recommending.

Like Ratio

Likes per reach. A 1K account with 100 likes beats a 100K account with 200.

Weekly Content Mix

3-4 ReelsDiscovery & reach (2.25x more reach than photos)
1-2 CarouselsHighest engagement & saves
1-2 StaticsGrid aesthetic & brand consistency
Daily StoriesPolls, questions, behind-the-scenes

Reel Hooks (First 3 Seconds = Everything)

"I paid $8 for this, and watch what it looks like styled..."
"Would you have walked past this at the thrift store?"
"Everyone says you can't mix old and new. Watch this."
"Here's how I turn a $5 find into the centerpiece of a room"

TikTok

Where strangers discover you & fall in love

Content Types That Go Viral

Come Thrift V. High

The hunt, the finds, the moment of discovery, the styled result at home.

Before & After V. High

Show "after" first, cut to "before," show the process. Gets replayed.

Styling Process High

Real-time shelf/table styling with trending sound or soft folk music.

Story Time High

Talk to camera while arranging. Where the piece came from, what it means.

Home Tours High

Slow pan with text overlays: "1952 brass candlesticks / Modern Target throw"

Design Tips High

"3 things I look for at every estate sale" — text + voiceover.

Key Rules

5-7x/wkTikTok rewards volume more than Instagram
2-4 sec hook40% higher completion rates with strong hooks
Show faceAccounts with face-on-camera grow 3x faster
No watermarksNever repost TikToks to IG with watermarks
